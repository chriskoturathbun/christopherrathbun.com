The hardest part of caring from a distance is the not knowing. Reminders closes that gap — a dependable daily call, and an instant alert the moment something's off.

How it works
01

Set the medicines & times

Add each medication and when it's taken. We automatically group medicines that can be taken together into the fewest calls.

02

We call — right on time

A warm AI voice calls at each scheduled time: "Hi Rose, it's time to take your morning medicine." Every day, never late.

03

You're told instantly

If they have a question, need help, or miss a call, we detect it and alert you and the emergency contact at once.

Peace of mind

If anything's wrong,
you'll know in seconds.

Every call is understood, not just placed. The moment your loved one sounds confused, asks for help, or doesn't pick up, we reach you.

  • Email + text alert to you and the emergency contact
  • A short summary of what they said, with the call recording
  • A missed call is an alert too — silence never goes unnoticed
