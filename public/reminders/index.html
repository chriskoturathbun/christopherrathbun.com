How it works

Step 1

Tell us the medicines & times

Add each medication and when it should be taken. We automatically group medicines that can be taken together.

Step 2

We call — on time, every time

A warm AI voice calls at each scheduled time: "Hi Rose, it's time to take your morning medicine."

Step 3

You're alerted to anything

If they have a question or need help, we detect it and alert you and the emergency contact by email and text.

Calls are recorded for quality and safety. Reminders is a supportive tool and is not a substitute for professional medical care or emergency services.
