Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Reminders is an AI voice service that places daily phone calls to remind people to take their medication. This policy explains, in plain language, what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We wrote it to be readable — if anything is unclear, please email us at privacy@christopherrathbun.com.

Information we collect

To set up and run a reminder schedule, we collect the patient's name, phone number, and timezone, along with the medications we should remind them about — names, doses, and the times each should be taken. We also collect details for the person who set up the account (the purchaser) and the emergency contact, such as name, email, and phone number. When you sign up, we record your consent, including a timestamp and the IP address from which it was given, so we have a clear record that permission was granted.

Because the reminders happen over the phone, we also collect the recordings and transcripts of those calls, which are created and stored as described below.

How the calls work

At each scheduled time, an AI voice agent places an outbound call to the patient. These calls are placed, recorded, and transcribed by our calling provider, Bland AI. The resulting transcript is then analyzed by an AI provider (OpenAI) for a single, narrow purpose: to detect whether the person needs help or has a question during the call. We do not use the recordings or transcripts to build advertising profiles or for any purpose unrelated to operating the reminder service.

Who we share data with

We share your information only with the service providers we need to operate Reminders: our calling provider (Bland AI), our AI analysis provider (OpenAI), and our email and SMS providers used to deliver alerts. Each provider receives only the information needed to perform its role. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Alerts to your contacts

When our analysis detects that the patient needs help or has a question, or when a scheduled call is missed, we notify the purchaser and the emergency contact by email and SMS so someone can follow up. These alerts may include relevant context from the call so the recipient understands why they're being contacted.

Data retention and deletion

We keep call recordings and transcripts only as long as needed to operate the service — to place reminders, generate alerts, and let you review recent activity. When data is no longer needed for those purposes, it is removed. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@christopherrathbun.com, and we will delete it unless we're required to retain certain records by law.

Your consent and choices

Reminders only places calls to numbers for which consent was captured at signup, along with the timestamp and IP address of that consent. You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing us or updating your account, which stops all future calls to that number. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of calls placed before the withdrawal.

Children

Reminders is intended for adults. The service is not directed at children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child's information has been provided to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

Not medical advice

Reminders is a supportive reminder tool. It is not a medical device, not medical advice, and not a substitute for professional medical care or emergency services. It does not diagnose, treat, or monitor any medical condition. In an emergency, call 911.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above, and significant changes may be communicated to you directly. Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to privacy@christopherrathbun.com.
