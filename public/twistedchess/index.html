Twisted Chess

Chess as you know it — then, after every move, you turn a quarter of the board 90°. Outwit your opponent across a board that never holds still.

You'll play White. Share the link with a friend to start.

How Twisted Chess works
  1. Make any legal chess move (all pieces move normally — castling, en passant, promotion included).
  2. Then twist one of the four 4×4 quadrants 90° clockwise or counter-clockwise. Every piece in that quadrant rotates with it.
  3. A twist can't leave your own king in check.
  4. You win by delivering checkmate at your opponent's move stage — or on the clock.
A game by Christopher Rathbun
