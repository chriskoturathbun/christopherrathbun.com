ClaudeApprove Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

  • Anonymous account token — a random identifier created when you tap Set Up. No name, email, or sign-in.
  • Push tokens — your device's Apple push token, used solely to deliver approval notifications.
  • Approval requests — the tool name, a one-line summary (e.g. a shell command), and the working directory path that Claude Code asks to run. These exist to show you what you're approving.

Retention

Pending approval requests expire after 10 minutes. Resolved requests are pruned on a rolling basis (recent history only). Pairing codes expire after 10 minutes and are deleted on use.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no third-party data sharing.
  • Notification content is sent only to Apple Push Notification service for delivery to your devices.
  • We never see your code or files — only the one-line summary of the action Claude Code requests.

Deleting your data

Delete the app and remove ~/.claude/watch-approve on your Mac to sever the pairing; server-side data for inactive accounts is purged periodically.
